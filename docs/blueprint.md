# **App Name**: Sarn Solar Solutions

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display Sarn Solar's mission, values, and services with a visually appealing layout.
- Service Catalog: Detailed listing of solar services offered by Sarn Solar (installation, maintenance, consultation).
- Project Portfolio: Showcase completed solar projects with images, descriptions, and client testimonials.
- Cost Savings Estimator: AI tool that estimates potential cost savings and environmental impact with Sarn Solar's solutions.
- Contact Form: Easy-to-use form for customers to request quotes or more information.
- Blog Section: Share updates, insights, and educational content about solar energy and Sarn Solar's expertise.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A bright, clean blue (#42A5F5), evoking reliability and the sky.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E3F2FD), creating a clean, open feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant green (#66BB6A) to highlight eco-friendly aspects and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a versatile sans-serif font.
- Use modern, flat icons to represent solar services and benefits.
- Clean and responsive design with clear sections and ample white space to highlight key information.
- Subtle animations for scrolling and loading to enhance user experience.
